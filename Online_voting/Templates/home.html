Welcome to the Online Voting System

This is the homepage with an introduction to the voting system.

About Online Polls

Online polls allow users to participate in voting through the internet, providing convenience and accessibility.

Types of Polls

There are various types of polls, including public opinion polls, election polls, and customer satisfaction surveys.

Process of Polling Questions

Users can create polls, add questions, and manage poll options through the online voting system interface.

How to Vote

To vote in a poll, users select their choices from the provided options and submit their votes securely.
